Sustainable Design

Sustainable Design: As our name implies, design2 LAST inc is focused on providing sustainable solutions for all of our projects. As members of the US Green Building Council and International Living Future Institute, we are passionate about the environmental, social and health impact of all of our projects. We have experience managing LEED and Living Building Challenge projects and can provide LEED consultation and documentation for any project type. Our specialty is in the use and detailing of appropriate sustainable materials, building systems, and technologies that optimize design quality within established budgets, and meet or exceed sustainability goals

  • LEED Documentation: design2 LAST, inc can provide LEED Administration services to architecture firms, agencies, and contractors, for both design and construction phases of a project.
  • Living Building Challenge (LBC): design2 LAST is a member of the International Living Future Institute and has worked on an LBC project in Alaska. The LBC is known as the most ambitious green building rating system in the industry today. If you are up to the challenge, we are ready to work with you to achieve this admirable goal.
  • Material Research and Selection: Providing healthier, non-toxic, materials in your building is our passion. We are always looking for the best thing on the market that will provide our clients with peace of mind once they move in. We have done a significant amount of research on building materials for our projects and can assist agencies and clients to make the right material choices for their facilities.
