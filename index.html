Design 2 Last

Firm Overview

design2 LAST inc is a full service architecture firm specializing in commercial, Industrial, and institutional design for both new construction and renovation projects. Our mission is to provide design excellence, stewardship of our natural and built environment, and exceptional client service. Our philosophy is centered on the premise that design excellence should be reflected in each and every aspect of a building: its fit within the community and environment, its function and relationship to its users, its physical manifestation, its inner workings, and its cost. We have extensive experience working on federal, state and local government projects across the US. We hold current licenses in Washington, Alaska, Texas, Nevada and Guam. NCARB certification allows us to easily obtain reciprocity in other jurisdictions as project opportunities demand. design2 LAST inc is committed to Building Information Modeling (BIM) and can provide project files in Revit Architecture or in AutoCAD.

Leadership

design2 LAST inc knows you, our client, are the most important team member. We listen first, and then work with you to develop solutions that respond to your culture and business objective. Our reputation is built on client satisfaction. Over years in business, we have built trusting and lasting teaming relationships with highly qualified and responsive consultants. Our project management approach begins with our unique ability to organize our team, keep moving forward, and remain focused on the program goals and requirements. Frequent client interaction throughout the process results in successful projects on schedule and on budget.

Sustainability

As the name implies, design2 LAST inc is focused on providing sustainable solutions for all of our projects. As members of the US Green Building Council and International Living Future Institute, we have experience managing LEED and Living Building Challenge projects. We can provide LEED consultation and documentation for any project type. Ourspecialty is in the use and detailing of appropriate sustainable materials, building systems, and technologies that optimize design quality within established budgets and meet or exceed sustainability goals.
