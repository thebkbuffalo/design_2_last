Our Firm

Our Firm design2 LAST is a full service architecture firm specializing in commercial, Industrial, and institutional design for both new construction and renovation projects. Our mission is to provide design excellence, stewardship of our natural and built environment, and exceptional client service. Our philosophy is centered on the premise that design excellence should be reflected in each and every aspect of a building: its fit within the community and environment, its function and relationship to its users, its physical manifestation, its inner workings, and its cost. We have extensive experience working on federal, state and local government projects across the US. We hold current licenses in Washington, Alaska, Texas, Nevada and Guam. NCARB certification allows us to easily obtain reciprocity in other jurisdictions as project opportunities demand. We are committed to Building Information Modeling (BIM) and can provide project files in Revit Architecture or in AutoCAD.

Woman Owned Business: design2 LAST is a current certificate holder as a Women's Business Enterprise (WBE) for the State of Washington program. To be eligible, a firm must be 51 percent owned by women, control the business enterprise, and illustrate clear expertise in the industry being certified. Our Certification Number is W2F0022838.

Disadvantaged Business Enterprise: design2 LAST is in compliance with Title 49 Code of Federal Regulation (CFR), Part 26 and remains certified as a Disadvantaged Business Enterprise (DBE) under the Federal Program. Our Certification Number is D2F0022838.

Being certified DBE/WBE allows our clients to support diversity in the marketplace with the peace-of-mind that they are working with well qualified professionals.

Federal WOSB and EDWOSB certification: In accordance with the small business administration requirements, we have self-certified as WOSB and EDWOSB and uploaded all the required documents to the federal repository. We can give access to these documents to agency clients as necessary by request.
Leadership
Lauri Strauss, AIA, LEED AP, BD&C President CEO, Founder- Lauri founded design2 LAST in 2012. She shares her enthusiasm, knowledge and expertise in design quality, skills in project management, and sustainability goals with new and repeat clients from all across the US. She brings to each project over 25 years of professional experience in architecture; managing numerous project types for municipal, county, state and federal government clients. Her passion for sustainable design began in 1993 when she was pursuing her Master’s degree in Architecture. It has led her to design dynamic, high performing and low impact buildings. She has established relationships with some of the most influential leaders in the green building community. Lauri’s passion for learning and earth-saving idealism led her to pursue and recently obtain a second Master’s degree. In 2013 she received a Master of Science in Green Building from the San Francisco Institute of Architecture. Lauri was awarded a Fellow of the Cascadia Green Building Council in 2012 and continually seeks state of the art sustainable technologies, education and information.
